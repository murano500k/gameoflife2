Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 31, 2026

Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Game of Life ("we", "us", or "the App") handles information when you use our mobile application. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information.

Game of Life is designed with privacy in mind. The App:

  • Does not collect personal data
  • Does not require account registration
  • Does not track your location
  • Does not access your contacts, camera, or microphone
  • Does not require any device permissions

Data Storage

All game data (grid state, settings, patterns) is stored locally in your device's memory only and is:

  • Never transmitted to external servers
  • Never shared with third parties
  • Automatically cleared when you close the App
  • Not backed up to cloud services

Third-Party Services

Game of Life does not integrate with any third-party services, including:

  • Analytics platforms
  • Advertising networks
  • Social media SDKs
  • Crash reporting services
  • Cloud storage providers

Internet Access

The App operates entirely offline and does not require or use internet connectivity.

Children's Privacy

Game of Life does not collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. The App is safe for users of all ages.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date at the top of this document. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.

Your Rights

Since we do not collect any personal data, there is no personal information to access, modify, or delete. You have complete control over your experience with the App.

Summary

Game of Life is a simple, offline cellular automaton simulation that respects your privacy by design. We believe great software doesn't need to collect your data.
